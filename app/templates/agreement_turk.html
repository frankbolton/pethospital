Some Task Particulars.

Objectives

The objective of this study is to better understand human factors of intelligent notification systems in the context of performing a process management task.

Action Required

The cat health levels tend to decrease over time, and sometimes a mobile phone message will give a warning about a problem with one of the stations.
You will view cat health levels, and heal the cat's as you think it's necessary.
Viewing and healing the cats cost you some score.
Your objective is to keep all of the health levels above 0%.

Benefits and possible risks

The main personal benefit from your participation is the payment you will receive for participation. A performance bonus will be issued to one player and the chances of winning are based on your score.
Apart from that, the knowledge received may be of value to humanity and hopefully the development of further mobile phone notification mechanisms.
You will be performing a process management task that is quite demanding. You may experience tiredness at the end of the study, but no parts of the exam are harmful.

Rights
