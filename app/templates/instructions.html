Instructions to perform the study

Your objective is to apply yourself to maximize your score in each session. The higher your score- the greater your odds of winning a $5 bonus!

Method

To have your health increase, all of the stations must have a positive health level and the health of all the stations must be hidden.
The pet health levels decrease over time.
Topping up the health is done by pressing the heal "button" - don't do it excessively as it's costly to your score.

Study particulars

Before the study you will perform a {{learntime}} minute training block to show you how the system behaves.
The study will consist of {{numberOfSessions}} tasks of {{exptime}} minutes each.
After each task you have a chance to answer a few questions about your experience.
Please answer these honestly based on your personal experience, there are no incorrect answers.
This makes for an allocated experiment time of {{totalTime}} minutes.
