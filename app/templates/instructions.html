Instructions to perform the study

Your objective is to apply myself to try maximise the score in each session. The higher the score- the greater your odds of winning a $10 bonus!

Method

All of the stations must a positive heath level, and the health values hidden for your score to increase.
The pet health levels decrease over time.
Topping up the health is done by pressing the heal "button" - don't do it except when neccessary as it's costly.

Experiment particulars

The experiment will consist of 5 tasks of 5 minutes each.
After each task you have a chance to answer a few questions about your experience.
This makes for an allocated experiment time of 30 minutes.
