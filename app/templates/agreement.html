The Study Procedure

Objectives

My objective is to apply myself to try maximise the score in each session.
The experiment objectives are to understand better human factors of intelligent notification systems in the context of performing a process management task.

Method

I will try to maximise the score, the score increases when all of the pet stations have a positive heath level.
The pet health levels tend to decrease over time, and sometimes a mobile phone message will give a warning about a problem with one of the stations.
Looking inside the station, and healing the station costs me some score.
I will try to learn the behaviour, to keep all the stations healthy, without looking and healing too often.

Experiment particulars

The experiment will consist of 5 tasks of 5 minutes each. After each task you have a chance to answer a few questions about your experience. This makes for an allocated experiment time of 30 minutes.
The experiment will take place in the HCI experiment lab at Tel Aviv University. Up to 8 participants will perform the experiment at once and a total of 30 participants will be involved in each experiment.

Benefits and possible risks

The main personal benefit from your participation is the payment I will receive for participation. This will include a basic amount of 40 sheckels and a performance bonus of up to 100 shekels that will be a score based lottery.
Apart from that, the knowledge received may be of value to humanity and hopefully the development of further mobile phone notification mechanisms.

You will be performing a process management task that is quite demanding. You may experience tiredness at the end of the experiment, but no parts of the exam are harmful.
