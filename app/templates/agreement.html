Objectives

The objective of this study is to better understand human factors of intelligent notification systems in the context of performing a process management task.

The main personal benefit from your participation is the payment you will receive for participation. You will receive a basic amount of {{basicPay}} and a bonus of {{bonusPay}} based on how well you perform the task.
